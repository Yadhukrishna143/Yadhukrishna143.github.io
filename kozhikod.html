BEYPUR BEACH
Beypore beach is a combination of a sea beach, a promenade cum waterbreak and the signature backwaters of Kerala.
ELATHUR BEACH
The Arabian Sea lies to the west of Elathur. The Elathur beach stretches from Puthiyanirathe to Korapuzha. Marine mussels are abundant in this part of the sea.
VELLARIMALA
Vellarimala Hills is a mountain range in Kerala, India, spread across Thiruvambady Panchayat in Kozhikode district and Meppadi Panchayat in Wayanad district
